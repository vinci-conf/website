Important Notes:

  • Every accepted paper or poster must be covered by at least one author's registration to be included in the symposium proceedings.
  • Regular and student registration includes technical sessions, proceedings, lunches, and coffee breaks.

Registration Form

For conference registration, please send an email to michael.burch@fhgr.ch with your full name, ACM membership, address, country, and email address. We will contact you as soon as possible with the bank account details, in case you have to pay a registration fee. If your paper is accepted to the Youth Academic Workshop, the registration costs are 100 CHF for the student, 150 CHF for late registration. We will also send a registration confirmation and also a conference attendance confirmation at your given address. Please contact michael.burch@fhgr.ch for further details or missing information.
